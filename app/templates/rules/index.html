Metrics Guide

Support (A→B): P(A∪B) - How often A and B appear together.

Confidence (A→B): P(B|A) - Probability of B given A was purchased.

Lift (A→B): Confidence / P(B) - Correlation strength.

  • Lift > 1: Positive correlation
  • Lift = 1: Independent
  • Lift < 1: Negative correlation
